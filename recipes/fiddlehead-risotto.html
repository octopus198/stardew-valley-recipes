Fiddlehead Risotto

Description

A creamy rice dish served with sauteed fern heads. It's a little bland.

It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit.

If you want to befriend Kent and Clint, gift them this dish because they love it.

Ingredients

  • Fiddlehead Fern
  • Oil
  • Garlic

Recipes instructions

  1. Obtain recipe: btain recipe: watch The Queen of Sauce on TV on Fall 28, Year 2.
  2. Obtain Fiddlehead Fern:
    • Foraging in the Secret Woods during Summer (78%).
    • Foraging in the Prehistoric floors of the Skull Cavern.
    • Foraging on levels 41-69 of the Mines while the Shrine of Challenge is active.
    • Foraging in the Ginger Island jungle in all seasons.
    • Occasionally at the Traveling Cart for 270 to 1,000g.
  3. Obtain Oil: the easiest way is to buy from Pierre's General Store for 200g.
  4. Obtain Garlic: buy Garlic Seeds from Pierre's General Store from Year 2 for 40g, grow it in 4 days.
