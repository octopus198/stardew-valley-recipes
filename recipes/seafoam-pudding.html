Seafoam Pudding

Description

This briny pudding will really get you into the maritime mindset!

It is prepared using either the kitchen inside an upgraded farmhouse or a Cookout Kit.

You can purchase this dish for 5,000g from the shop at the Dance of the Moonlight Jellies festival each year. It may also randomly appear at the Traveling Cart for 900 to 1,500g.

Ingredients

  • Flounder
  • Midnight Carp
  • Squid Ink

Recipes instructions

  1. Obtain recipe: Buy advanced fishing rods from Willy and go fishing a lot to reach level 9 of Fishing. After that, you will obtain the recipe for Seafoam Pudding.
  2. Catch a Flounder: go fishing in the ocean at The Beach during Spring and Summer, from 6am to 8pm.
  3. Catch a Midnight Carp: go fishing in the mountain lake and the Cindersap Forest pond during Fall and Winter late at night, from 10pm to 2am.
  4. Catch a Squid: go fishing in the ocean at The Beach in the evening during Winter, from 6pm to 2am.
  5. Get Squid Ink: construct a Fish Pond (go to Robin's house) for the Squid and wait for the Pond to drop Squid Ink.
